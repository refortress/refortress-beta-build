                   ========================
                      ShutDown II
                   ========================

 OBJECTIVE:
 ====================
 Deactivate the enemy's security at 
 their control center.  

 Once deactivated, grab their flag, 
 and run it back to your capture point.

 Security will re-activate after 75 
 seconds.

 SCORING:
 ================
 10 points to the player & Team for 
 capturing the flag.
