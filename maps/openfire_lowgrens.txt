Open Fire - Capture The Flag

Objective: Enter the enemy Base and
deactivate the lasers which guard access
to their Flag. Grab the flag and bring it
back to your Base. Place it on the glass
square to Capture it. 

Scoring: 10 points per Capture.

Other Notes: Flag carriers drop the Flag when
they die. Dropped flags return to their Base
after 60 seconds. Lasers stay deactivated
for only 30 seconds.

(This is the League edition)
