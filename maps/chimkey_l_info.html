Known Bugs I spent a very long time devising a way of stopping a stupid bug which made an invisible ledge above the balconies on each base. In the end I made clip walls direct you away from it so thats why they are there. If you think there are any other errors then they are probably as I intended it.

Designer's Notes
This map is designed to be a fast paced and relatively high scoring map. I have tried to keep r_speeds to a minimum to help people with low end PCs - they only go over 500 in one place and average about 300. The rocks were a bast to make - I used 4 sided polygons which are kinda tricky. Anyway hope you enjoy it.
